Simple Present (Do/Does)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + verbo + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + aux (do/does not)+ verbo + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + aux do/does + sujeto + verbo + complemento.

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I Work I don't Work Do I Work?
You You Work You don't Work Do You Work?
He He Works He doesn't Work Does He Work?
She She Works She doesn't Work Do es She Work?
It It Works It doesn't Work Does It Work?
We We Work We don't Work Do We Work?
They They Work They don't Work Do They Work?

Examples

The sun rises in the east and sets in the west.

El sol sale en el este y se pone en el oeste.

My mother never watches tv in her Room

Mi mamá nunca ve tv en su cuarto

Paul never does his homework

Paul nunca hace su tarea.

Do you usually watch movies in the afternoon?

Normalmente ves peliculas en la noche?

My dad doesn't like sushi

A mi papá no le gusta el sushi.

Nick travels to Australia every Summer.

Nick viaja a Australia todos los veranos.

kate speaks English and German.

kate habla ingles y aleman.

Harry goes to school by bus.

Harry va a la escuela en autobus.

She works for NASA

Ella trabaja para la NASA.

I don't drink coffe

Yo no bebo cafe.

Where does Jane come from?

De donde viene Jane?
