Continuos present perfect (have/has+been)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + verb (have/has) + been + verb(ing)+ complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + verb (have not/has not)+ been + verb(ing) + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + (Have/Has) + Sujeto + been + verb(ing)+ complemento.

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I Have been listening to music. I Have not been working here long. Have I been studying enough.
She/He Has been listening to music. Has not been working here long. Has She/He been studying enough.
We/You/They Have been listening to music. Have not been working here long. Have We/You/They been studying enough.

Examples

It has been raining all day

Ha estado lloviendo todo el dia.

I have been working as a waiter since june.

He estado trabajando como camarero desde junio.

Have you been waiting long?

Has estado esperando mucho?

We Have been talking for hours

Hemos estado hablando durante horas.

She hasn't been feeling well recently.

No se ha sentido bien recientemente.

I have been thinking about you lately.

He estado pensando en ti ultimamente.

I have been looking for you all day.

Te he estado buscando todo el dia.

What have you been doing recently?

Que has estado haciendo ultimamente?

We have been going out for two months now.

Hemos estado saliendo por dos meses.

How long has it been snowing?

Cuanto tiempo ha estado nevando?
