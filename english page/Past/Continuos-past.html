Past Continuos(Was/Were +ing)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + was/were + (verbo+ing) + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + wasn't/weren't + (verbo+ing) + complemento

Interrogativa

Was/Were + sujeto + (verbo+ing) + complemento

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Interrogative
I I was playing I wasn't playing Was I playing?
He He was playing He wasn't playing Was He playing?
She She was playing She wasn't playing Was She playing?
It It was playing It wasn't playing Was It playing?
You You were playing You weren't playing Were You playing?
We We were playing We weren't playing Were We playing?
They They were playing They weren't playing were They playing?

Examples

The sun was shining

El sol estaba brillando

She was only trying to help us.

Ella solo estaba tratando de ayudarnos.

Barbara was reading comics.

Barbara estaba leyendo comics.

I waswondering if you could come with us.

Yo me estaba preguntando si podias venir con nostros.

I was not joking

Yo no estaba bromeando.

She was not listening to you

Ella no estaba escuchandote

I wasn't feeling well yesterday

Yo no me sentia bien ayer.

Was He telling the truth?

El estabadiciendo la verdad?

Where was she going?

A donde estaba llendo ella?

Who wasyelling?

Quien estaba gritando?
