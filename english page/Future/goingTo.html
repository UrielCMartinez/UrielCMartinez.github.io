Going To (to be future/past )

Afirmativa

Sujeto + verbo (to be) + going to + verb infinitive + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + verbo (to be) + not + going to + verb infinitive + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + verbo (to be) + Sujeto + going to + verb infinitive + complemento+?

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I am going to eat a sandwich tonight. I am not going to go to the cinema. Am I going to write the email?
He/ She/ It is going to eat a sandwich tonight. is not going to go to the cinema. is He/She/It going to write the email?
You/ They/ We are going to eat a sandwich tonight. are not going to go to the cinema. are You/They/We going to write the email?

Examples (Future)

Noah is going to wash the dishes after the party.

Noah va a lavar los platos despues de la fiesta.

Madison is not going to buy that car.

Madison no va comprar ese auto.

Wendy is going to visit a friend in the evening.

Wendy va a visitar a una amiga esta noche.

My parents are not going to travel in the summer.

Mis padres no van a viajar en el verano.

He is going to speak at the weekly meeting.

El va ha hablar en la reunion semanal.

My father is going to change jobs.

Mi padre va a cambiar de trabajo.

I am going to wash my hands.

Yo voy a lavarme las manos.

Jack is going to prepare for the test.

Jack se va a preparar para el examen.

What are you going to do about that?

Que vas a hacer al respecto?

Are you friends going to help us?

Tus amigos van a ayudarnos?

Examples(Past)

I was going to enter the competition, but I got sick.

Iba a entrar a la competicion pero me enferme.

Mary was going to visit us, but she could not.

Mary iba a visitarnos pero no pudo.

I was going to call my mother when she arrived.

Iba a llamar a mi madre cuando ella llego.

I was going to play tenis, but it was too cold.

yo iba a jugar al tenis, pero hacia mucho frio.

We were going to work out outdoors when it started raining

Ibamos ha hacer ejercicio al aire libre cuando empezo a llover.
