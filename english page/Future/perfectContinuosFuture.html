Perfect Continuos Future (will+have+been)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + aux (Will) + (have+been) + verbo gerundio + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + aux (Will not) + (have+ been) + verbo gerundio + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + aux (Will) + Sujeto + (have+ been) + verbo gerundio + complemento.

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I Will have been dancing until sunrise. I Will not have been dancing... Will I have been dancing...?
He/ She/ It Will has been dancing until sunrise. Will not has been dancing... Will He/She/it has been dancing...?
You/ They/ We Will have been dancing until sunrise. Will not have been dancing... Will You/They/We have been dancing...?

Examples

I will have been working for my uncle.

Yo habre estado trabajando para mi tio.

She will have been finishing her homework.

Ella habra estado terminando su tarea.

We will have been talking for more than two hours..

Nosotros habremos estado hablando por mas de dos horas.

They will have been completing the project.

Ellos habran estado completando el proyecto.

I will have been cooking something special for you.

yo habre estado cocinando algo especial para ti.

I will have been working in the firm form many years.

Yo habre estado trabajando en esa empresa por muchos años.

We will have been loving each other for twenty years.

Nosotros nos habremos estando amando uno al otro por veinte años.

You will have been investing in something revolutionary.

Tu habras estado invirtiendo en algo revolucionario.

He will have been reading the book.

El habra estado leyendo el libro.

You will have been sleeping for more than fourteen hours.

Tu habras estado durmiendo por mas de 14 horas.
