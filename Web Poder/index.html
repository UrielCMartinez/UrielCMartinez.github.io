48th. Power rules

I

Never overshadow your hand.

You need to make your superiors feel like superiors, and you can achieve this by not showing off in ways that make them feel insecure.

To gain power, you need to make them look better and brighter than you.

II

NEVER TRUST YOUR FRIENDS TOO MUCH, LEARN TO USE YOUR ENEMIES.

Eventually, your friends betray you. This is a fact and you should never trust it too much. Instead of leaning on your friends, keep your former enemies close as they tend to be more loyal.

III

HIDE YOUR INTENTIONS

To have true power, you need to be able to leave people uninformed. The easiest way to do this is to never show your true intentions. They will never be able to anticipate against you. You can also disorient them.

IV

ALWAYS SAY LESS THAN NECESSARY

When you're trying to impress someone, the more you talk, the more ordinary you seem and the less control you have. It is important to be vague and talk as little as possible. Those who say little seem more powerful and intimidate people with their discretion.

V

ALMOST EVERYTHING DEPENDS ON YOUR PRESTIGE. DEFEND HIM TO THE DEATH.

Your reputation is the basis of your power. The more recognized and respected you are, the more powerful you will be. A good reputation is already an advantage, but if you want to destroy your enemies, do it by attacking their reputations.

VI

SEEK TO CATCH ATTENTION AT ANY PRICE

People are judged by their appearance and if you want to have power, then not attracting people's attention can set you up for failure. You should always seek attention from people and not be invisible. Be mysterious, but differ from the crowd to stand out.

VII

GET OTHERS TO WORK FOR YOU, BUT DON'T STOP TAKING THE LAURLES

If you want to be powerful, you need to be able to take credit for the work of others. Use the people who work for you and when possible take credit for the activities they do. Never let others take credit for your work. This will make them think that you are the master of efficiency.

VIII

MAKE PEOPLE COME TOWARDS YOU AND IF NECESSARY, USE THE MOST APPROPRIATE BAIT TO ACHIEVE IT

You need to make people seek you out because it gives you power and control. It's much better if your opponents come to you and you never look for them. When they come to you, they abandon their own plans and follow yours. Make promises of big profits and when you attract them, attack.

IX

WIN THROUGH YOUR ACTIONS, NEVER THROUGH ARGUMENTS

You don't gain anything from arguments because they generate resentment in the loser which harms your ability to influence. To win, you need to take actions that communicate what you want. Instead of explaining something, demonstrate how it should be done.

X

DANGER OF CONTAGION: AVOID THE LOSERS AND THE UNHAPPY

Being around unpleasant and sad people drains your emotional and mental capacity. These people contaminate you.

XI

MAKE PEOPLE DEPEND ON YOU

To maintain control, you need people to depend on you. The more people need you, the more freedom you will have. Make people want to come to you. Never teach them what to do so as not to depend on you.

XII

TO DISARM YOUR VICTIM, USE FRANKNESS AND GENEROSITY SELECTIVELY

A sincere action is capable of hiding your dishonest intentions. When you are honest, you will lower people's defenses and they will start to trust you. Make them trust you before attacking.

XIII

WHEN YOU ASK FOR HELP, NEVER APPEAL TO PEOPLE'S COMPASSION OR GRATITUDE BUT TO THEIR SELFISHNESS

If you need help, you cannot be humble and depend on the compassion of others. You must find something that will benefit that person by helping you and emphasize it so as not to expose yourself and achieve your goal.

XIV

SHOW YOURSELF LIKE A FRIEND, BUT ACT LIKE A SPY

Approach people to get information and ask intrusive questions. Take every opportunity to get more information. Deceive people to appear close, gain their trust and learn as much as you can.

XV

COMPLETELY CRUSH YOUR ENEMY

Harming your enemy is not enough. The important thing is to destroy it, so that it does not rise again. Attack it in mind and spirit and never feel sorry until you finish it.

XVI

USE ABSENCE TO INCREASE RESPECT AND HONOR

The more you are present, the less your value will be. If you are part of a group, stand back and let people notice your distance.

XVII

KEEP THE SUSPENSE. HANDLE THE ART OF THE UNPREDICTABLE

Being predictable gives power to others. If you act in an unpredictable and inconsistent manner, people will stop trying to predict your moves. The more unpredictable you are, the more people will be intimidated about your next moves.

XVIII

DO NOT BUILD FORTRESS TO PROTECT YOURSELF. ISOLATION IS DANGEROUS

If you want to be powerful, you can't isolate yourself. Isolation will not allow you to obtain the most important information. It's better if you infiltrate the group. You will be protected from your enemies.

XIX

KNOW WHO YOU ARE DEALING WITH: DON'T OFFEND THE WRONG PERSON

Never assume that everyone will respond the same way to your offenses. Think about who you want to attack and choose your victims carefully. Never let your offenses be misunderstood and reach an unplanned target.

XX

DON'T COMMIT TO ANYONE

If there is a conflict, fools rush to take sides. You must remain neutral and never compromise with others, only with yourself.

XXI

PRETEND CANDIDITY TO CATCH THE CANDID: SHOW YOURSELF DUMBER THAN YOUR VICTIM

People like to feel smart. Make your victims think they are smarter than you. If they believe this, they will never think they will have reason to distrust you.

XXII

USE THE TACTIC OF CAPITULATION. TRANSFORM WEAKNESS INTO POWER

If you are the weaker opponent, don't keep fighting, give up. When you give up, you will have more time to recover and wait for others' power to decrease. Don't let your opponent have the satisfaction of defeating you. Surrender, recover, wait for your chance and take revenge.

XXIII

CONCENTRATE YOUR STRENGTHS

Saving your energy by keeping them focused is your strong point. Focus and don't attack different points at the same time.

XXIV

PLAY THE ROLE OF THE PERFECT COURTESAN

The courtier feels at home in a world where everything revolves around politics and aptitude. He flatters, submits to his superiors and imposes his power invisibly. If you flatter the king, there are no limits to where you can go at court.

XXV

TRY TO RECREATE YOURSELF PERMANENTLY

Recreate yourself by creating a new identity that attracts the attention and interest of your audience. Be the architect of your image and don't let others define it for you.

XXVI

KEEP YOUR HANDS CLEAN

Create an exemplary facade and never engage in suspicious activities. If you have to do something bad, let others do it for you. You must communicate perfection and you must never engage in dubious activities. If you do it, blame someone else and come out with clean hands.

XXVII

PLAY WITH PEOPLE'S NEED TO BELIEVE IN SOMETHING, TO GET UNCONDITIONAL FOLLOWERS

People need to believe in something. Use this to your advantage and give them common cause to make you their leader. Communicate vaguely, make promises, demonstrate your superiority, and don't talk about a rational plan. Take advantage of the faith of others. Create rituals for your disciplines and ask them to make sacrifices for the cause.

XXVIII

BE BOLD TO TAKE ACTION

If you're not sure about something in particular, don't do it. Your doubts will hinder your execution. Take action with certainty and attract attention. Everyone admires the brave and bold.

XXIX

PLAN YOUR ACTIONS FROM START TO FINISH

The end is everything and you must plan each step, taking into account the possible consequences and obstacles that can nullify your efforts and ruin your plan. Don't let circumstances surprise you and know when to stop.

XXX

MAKE YOUR ACHIEVEMENTS APPEAR EFFORTLESS

Your feats should seem natural and easy. Ignore your efforts and never mention that something has cost you a lot of work. Always make it look natural and simple and give the appearance that you can do more. However, don't show anyone how you did it or your tactics will be used against you.

XXXI

CONTROL THE OPTIONS: MAKE OTHERS PLAY WITH THE CARDS YOU DEAL

Shuffle your victims and make them feel that you are in control. Give them options to choose from, but make sure all options meet your goals.

XXXII

PLAY WITH PEOPLE'S FANTASIES

Never turn to the truth unless you are prepared for indignation generated by disappointment. People want to romanticize their lives and if you manage to trick them using this, you will gain power.

XXXIII

DISCOVER THE ACHILLES HEEL OF OTHERS

Every person has weaknesses and it is up to you to explore them. A weakness can be fear, an addiction or a secret. If you know people's weaknesses, you can handle them as you see fit.

XXXIV

ACT LIKE A KING TO BE TREATED AS SUCH

The way you normally behave is how you will be treated. Sooner or later acting like a commoner will lead to you being ignored.

XXXV

MASTER THE ART OF OPPORTUNITY

Do not rush, as it announces a lack of control over yourself. Be patient, as if you are sure that things will turn out the way you want. Recognizes the correct time to act and is reserved when the moment is not appropriate.

XXXVI

BELIMINATE THE THINGS YOU CAN'T HAVE: IGNORING THEM IS THE BEST VENGEANCE

The more attention you give to your enemy, the more powerful you will make him, and a small mistake will be more serious and obvious when you try to fix it. If there is something you want and you cannot have it, show contempt for it, pretend that it has no value to you. The less interest you show, the clearer your superiority will be.

XXXVII

CREATE AWESOME SHOWS

Large symbolic gestures with a dramatic air to the audience generate a breeze of power. Put on shows for the people around you, full of images and symbols that reinforce your presence.

XXXVIII

THINK HOW YOU WANT, BUT BEHAVE LIKE OTHERS

It's not worth showing people that you're going against the status quo. They will only think that you want attention and that you think you are superior. It's safer to blend in with the crowd and use common sense. Keep your idealisms to yourself and the people closest to you.

XXXIX

TURN THE WATERS TO ENSURE GOOD FISHING

Anger and emotions damage your functioning, so you need to always be calm.

XL

BELIMINATE WHAT IS FREE

If something is free, you should be cautious. This is something that usually entails a favor that will be collected in the future. Keep your assets flowing, generosity is a sign of power.

XLI

AVOID IMITATING GREAT MEN

If you have to replace a great man, you will have to do twice as much as he accomplished to surpass his glory. Don't stay in anyone's shadow, create your own space.

XLII

DEAD THE DOG, THE RABIES IS GONE

Neutralizes the leader's influence to disperse the group. Do it by isolating it. If you can reach the source of the problem, the herd will disperse and you can take control.

XLIII

WORK ON THE MIND AND HEART OF OTHERS

To get others to help your purposes, it will be necessary to seduce and influence them. The person you seduce will be loyal and to seduce a person you will have to act on your weaknesses and your mind. Manipulate your emotions and be loved or feared.

XLIV

DISARM AND ANGRY WITH THE MIRROR EFFECT

The mirror reflects reality, but it is also the cause of illusions. When you expose your enemies, doing what they do, they become confused with your strategy. The mirror effect taunts them and causes them to react in an unplanned way, reflecting their weaknesses. Few resist the confusion and chaos caused by the power of the mirror.

XLV

PREACH THE NEED TO PRODUCE CHANGES, BUT NEVER CHANGE TOO MUCH AT ONCE

Everyone understands the need for change. but people are creatures of habit. Too many changes cause a stir. If you assume the position of authority, you must maintain respect for tradition and if a change is necessary, make it seem like a simple improvement, a mere improvement on a tradition.

XLVI

NEVER SHOW YOURSELF TOO PERFECT

Looking better than others is dangerous and appearing perfect is even worse. If you cause envy, you create silent enemies. Occasionally he exhibits a defect or confesses harmless vices, to appear more human.

XLVII

DON'T GO BEYOND YOUR ORIGINAL GOAL: WHEN YOU SUCCEED LEARN WHEN TO STOP

Achieving victory is one of the most dangerous moments. When you gain arrogance and excess confidence you can exceed your goal and by going too far you will gain more enemies than necessary. Don't let success dominate you. Set a goal and when you reach it, stop.

XLVIII

BE CHANGING IN YOUR FORM

By taking a physical, clear and visible form you make yourself vulnerable to attacks. Instead of taking a form that your enemy can assimilate, stay adaptable and flow like water. Never bet on stability, but on chaos.
