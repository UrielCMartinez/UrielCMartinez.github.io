Continuos Present (to be + ing)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + verbo (to be)+ verbo gerundio +ing + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + verbo (to be)+ not + verbo gerundio +ing + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + verbo (to be) + sujeto + verbo gerundio +ing + complemento.

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I am listening I am not listening Am I listening?
You You are listening You are not listening Are You listening?
He He is listening He is not listening is He listening?
She She is listening She is not listening is She listening?
It It is listening It is not listening is It listening?
We We are listening We are not listening Are We listening?
They They are listening They are not listening Are They listening?

Examples

I am Writing a poem now

Estoy escribiendo un poema ahora.

She is leaving tomorrow morning.

Ella se va mañana por la mañana.

We are building a house.

Nosotros estamos construyendo una casa.

Tom is teaching English at that high school.

Tom enseña ingles en ese instituto.

They are studying French at university.

Ellos estan estudiando frances en la universidad.

Peter is leaving outside.

Peter esta saliendo afuera.

You are making great effort.

Tu estas haciendo un gran esfuerzo.

They are telling Mary what happened yesterday.

Ellos le estan contando a Mary lo que ocurrio ayer.

I am watching tv right now.

Estoy viendo television ahora mismo.

She is playing volleyball this afternoon.

Ella estara jugando voleybol esta tarde.
