Perfect Continuos Future (will+have/has+been)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + aux (Will) + (have/has been) + verbo gerundio + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + aux (Will not) + (have/has been) + verbo gerundio + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + aux (Will) + Sujeto + (have/has been) + verbo gerundio + complemento.

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I Will have been dancing until sunrise. I Will not have been dancing... Will I have been dancing...?
He/ She/ It Will has been dancing until sunrise. Will not has been dancing... Will He/She/it has been dancing...?
You/ They/ We Will have been dancing until sunrise. Will not have been dancing... Will You/They/We have been dancing...?
