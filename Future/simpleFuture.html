Simple Future (Will)

Afirmativa

Sujeto+ aux (Will)+ verb infinitive + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + aux (Will not)+ verb infinitive + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + aux (Will) + Sujeto + verb infinitive + complemento?

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I Will play in the next... I Will not work in the night. Will I work tomorrow?
He/ She/ It Will play in the next... Will not work in the night. Will He/She/It work tomorrow?
You/ We/ They Will play in the next... Will not work in the night. Will You/We/They work tomorrow?
