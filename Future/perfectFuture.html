Perfect Future (will+have)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + aux (Will) + (have) + verbo participio + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + aux (Will not) + (have) + verbo participio + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + aux (Will) + Sujeto + (have) + verbo participio + complemento.

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I Will have finished University by next year. I Will not have finished University ... Will I have finished University...?
He/ She/ It Will has finished University by next year. Will not has finished University ... Will He/She/It has finished University...?
You/ We/ They Will have finished University by next year. Will not have finished University ... Will You/He/She have finished University...?

Examples

I will have finished the book by friday.

Habre terminado el libro para el viernes.

They will have arrived by now.

Ellos ya habran llegado.

He will have left by the time we arrive.

El se habra ido para cuando lleguemos.

She will have planned everything before the trip.

Ella habra planeado todo antes del viaje.

The party will have already started.

la fiesta ya habra comenzado.

We will not have arrived by then.

No habremos llegado para entonces.

He won't have bothered to go.

No se habra molestado en ir.

Sally won't have left home alredy.

Sally todavia no se habra ido de casa.

Who will arrived by then?

Quien habra llegado para ese entonces?

Will they have finished their course?

Habran terminado su curso?
