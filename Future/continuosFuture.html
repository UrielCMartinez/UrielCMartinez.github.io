Continuos Future (will be)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + Aux (Will be)+ verbo gerundio(ing) + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + aux (Will not be)+ verbo gerundio(ing) + complemento

Interrogativa

Wh + aux (Will)+ Sujeto + be + verbo gerundio(ing) + complemento.

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Question
I I Will be working next Tuesday I Will not be traveling tomorrow. Will I be reading a new book this weekend?
You You Will be working next Tuesday You Will not be traveling tomorrow. Will You be reading a new book this weekend?
He/ She/ It Will be working next Tuesday Will not be traveling tomorrow. Will He/She/It be reading a new book this weekend?
We/ They Will be working next Tuesday Will not be traveling tomorrow. Will We/They be reading a new book this weekend?
