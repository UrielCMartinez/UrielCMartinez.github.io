Perfect Continuos Conditional (would have been)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + would + have + been + present participle + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + would + have + not + been + present participle + complemento

Interrogativa

would + sujeto + have + been + present participle + complemento

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Interrogative
I I would have been living I wouldn't have been living would I have been living?
He He would have been living He wouldn't have been living would He have been living?
She She would have been living She wouldn't have been living would She have been living?
You You would have been living You wouldn't have been living would you have been living?
We We would have been living We wouldn't have been living would We have been living?
They They would have been living They wouldn't have been living would They have been living?

Examples

If the weather had been better (que no lo fue), I'd have been sitting in the garden when he arrived (pero no fue así).

If she hadn't got a job in London (cosa que sí consiguió), she would have been working in Paris (pero no fue así).

If I had had a ball I would have been playing football.

If I had known it was dangerous I wouldn't have been climbing that cliff.
