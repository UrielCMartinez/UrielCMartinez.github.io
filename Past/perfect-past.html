Past Perfect

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Interrogative
I I had met I had not met had I met?
He He was playing He wasn't playing Was He playing?
She She was playing She wasn't playing Was She playing?
It It was playing It wasn't playing Was It playing?
You You were playing You weren't playing Were You playing?
We We were playing We weren't playing Were We playing?
They They were playing They weren't playing were They playing?

Informacion

Afirmativa

Sujeto + auxiliar had + verbo en pasado + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + auxiliary had + not + verbo en pasado + complemento

Interrogativa

Auxiliar had + sujeto + verbo en pasado + complemento ?

Examples

The bus had just arrived to the station when I bought my ticket.

The cat had just jumped over the wall, before the dog could catch it .

It was the most difficult game I had ever played.

My father had visited China several times, so that's why he knows it so well.
