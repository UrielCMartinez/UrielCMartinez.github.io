Past Perfect (had)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + aux had + verbo en pasado + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + aux hadn't + verbo en pasado + complemento

Interrogativa

aux had + sujeto + verbo en pasado + complemento + ?

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Interrogative
I I had met I had not met had I met?
He He had playing He had not playing Had He playing?
She She had playing She had not playing Had She playing?
It It had playing It had not playing Had It playing?
You You had playing You had not playing Had You playing?
We We had playing We had not playing Had We playing?
They They had playing They had not playing Had They playing?

Examples

They had seen each other before.

Ellos ya se habian visto antes.

Ben had gone to bed at 10:PM.

Ben se habia ido a dormir a las 10:PM.

The police had searched high and low.

La policia habia buscado por todas partes.

I knew who had said that.

Sabia quien habia dicho eso.

I hadn't thought of it that way.

No lo habia pensado de esa manera.

Amy had never swum with sharks before

Amy nunca antes habia nadado con tiburones.

I hadn't been surfing before

No habia surfeado antes.

Had this happened before?

Habia pasado esto antes?

Where had they met?

Donde se habian conocido?

Had you listened to this song?

Habias escuchado esta cancion?
