Simple past (Was/Were)

Afirmativa

Sujeto + Was + verbo + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + Wasn't + verbo + complemento

Interrogativa

Was + sujeto + verbo en forma básica + complemento

Structure

Affirmative Negative Question
I I was I wasn't Was I?
He He was He wasn't Was He?
She She was She wasn't Was She?
It It was It wasn't Was It?
You You were You weren't Were You?
We We were We weren't Were We?
They They were They weren't were They?

Examples

He was at home when I called

El estaba en casa cuando lo llame

They were at the club yesterday

Ellos estuvieron en el club ayer

Why was not Diana there?

Porque Diana no estaba ahi?

The hotel was really comfortable

El hotel estuvo realmente confortable

She was already awake

Ella ya estaba despierta

I wasn't aslepp when she arrived.

No estaba dormido cuando ella llego.

Were not you 21 years old?

No Tenias 21 años?

The game was very funny

El juego estuvo muy divertido

I was angry, that is why I shouted

Estaba enojado, por eso grite.

This time last year I was in London

Hace un año exacto estaba en Londres
