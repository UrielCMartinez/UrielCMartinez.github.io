Perfect Conditional

Afirmativa

Sujeto + would have+ (past participle) + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + would not + (past participle) + complemento

Interrogativa

Would + Sujeto +have+ (past participle) + complemento + ?

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Interrogative
I I would have +(past participle) I wouldn't have +(past participle) Would I have +past participle?
He He would have +(past participle) He wouldn't have +(past participle) Would He have +past participle?
She She would have +(past participle) She wouldn't have +(past participle) Would She have +past participle?
It It would have +(past participle) It wouldn't have +(past participle) Would It have +past participle?
You You would have +(past participle) You wouldn't have +(past participle) Would You have +past participle?
We We would have +(past participle) We wouldn't have +(past participle) Would We have +past participle?
They They would have +(past participle) They wouldn't have +(past participle) Would They have +past participle?

Examples

I would have bought a car last year if I had had more money.

Would you have called me if you had known my phone number?

We would not have gone skiing if we had seen the weather forecast.
