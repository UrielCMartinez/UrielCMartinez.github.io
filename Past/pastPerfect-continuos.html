past perfect Continuos

Afirmativa

Sujeto + verbo auxiliar (had been) + (verbo+ing) + complemento

Negativa

Sujeto + had not been + (verbo+ing) + complemento

Interrogativa

Had + Sujeto + been + (verbo+ing) + complemento + ?

Estructura

Affirmative Negative Interrogative
I I had been playing I hadn't been playing Had I been playing?
He He had been playing He hadn't been playing had He been playing?
She She had been playing She hadn't been playing had She been playing?
It It had been playing It hadn't been playing had It been playing?
You You had been playing You hadn't been playing had You been playing?
We We had been playing We hadn't been playing had We been playing?
They They had been playing They hadn't been playing had They been playing?

Examples

She had been reading for seven hours when she fell asleep

How long had they been running when they took a break?

I was tired because I had been working all day
